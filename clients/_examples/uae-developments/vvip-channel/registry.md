## Channel structure

Four sub-channels, each with distinct protocol and gatekeeper conventions:

- **Ruling families / royal households** — by country and household; AD-anchored emphasis given the developer's home emirate
- **Government officials** — UAE federal cabinet, AD emirate-level cabinet, ministers of state, undersecretaries, senior judiciary, senior military
- **Foreign dignitaries** — accredited ambassadors and visiting heads of state / ministers
- **Sovereign / quasi-sovereign institutional principals** — sovereign wealth funds, central banks, major foundations (with AD-resident emphasis given home-emirate alignment)
