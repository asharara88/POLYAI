## 2026-04-29 — Dual-corridor brand positioning (Saadiyat-cultural + Yas-active)

- **Decision:** Position the developer's master-community portfolio as a dual-corridor offering (Saadiyat = cultural / institutional; Yas = active / family) rather than a single brand promise. The Saadiyat Reserve Heights launch leans Saadiyat-cultural; the next launch (Yas-side, planned for Q1 2027) will lean active / family. Cross-corridor owners (existing Yas owners interested in Saadiyat, or vice versa) become a high-value segment for both.
- **Rationale:** The portfolio's two flagship master communities serve genuinely different psychographic profiles even within the same wealth tier. Forcing one brand voice across both dilutes both. Dual-corridor positioning lets each launch's creative speak in its native register while the master brand stays unified at the institutional level.
- **Expected outcome:** Cross-corridor reservation rate (existing owner of one community buying into the other) tracked as a KPI starting Q3.
