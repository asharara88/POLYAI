# VVIP channel

Owned by `vvip-channel-enablement`. Files:
- `registry.md` — VVIP registry (ruling families, government officials, foreign dignitaries, other VVIPs)
- `protocol-library.md` — forms of address, ceremonial precedence, gift rules, hosting norms specific to the markets in scope
- `gatekeepers.md` (optional) — gatekeeper map when separated from the registry
- `screening/<vvip-id>/` — PEP / sanctions / FCPA screening records (sensitive; treat appropriately)
- `events/<event-id>/` — protocol-aware event invitation lists and outcomes

Used for clients whose audience or counterparty list includes ruling-family principals, ministerial / cabinet-level officials, senior judiciary or military, foreign heads of state or accredited diplomats, or sovereign-institution principals.

**Sensitivity and discretion:**
- Names of registered VVIPs and especially of gatekeepers are not casually circulated. Most teams do not store full personal contact details in the repo at all — keep contact-level data in the developer's PA / EA system.
- The default discretion stance is invisible-to-public: no press, no social, no marketing references.
- All entries should be PEP / sanctions / FCPA screened on entry and revisited annually.

Skip for clients without a VVIP-class counterparty surface.
