# Sales

In-house sales operations for the developer's direct-lead funnel. Distinct from `brokers/` (the external channel) and from `wealth-channels/` and `vvip-channel/` (the relationship channels).

Files:
- `rm-team.md` — in-house relationship-manager roster (the recipients of direct-marketing leads)
- `allocation-rules.md` — per-tier and per-unit-type allocation eligibility for broker-channel allocation requests
- `commission-grid.md` (optional) — commission tiers / referral fees by channel
- `overflow-policy.md` (optional) — explicit opt-in for routing direct leads to brokers when in-house capacity is exceeded
- `lead-routing-rules.md` (optional) — per-segment routing rules for direct leads

Owned (read + maintained) by `inbound-qualifier`, `account-executive`, and `broker-enablement` collectively, depending on the file. Sales operations / Head of Sales is the human owner.
