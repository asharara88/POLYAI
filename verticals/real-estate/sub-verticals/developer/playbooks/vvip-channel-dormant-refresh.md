# Play — VVIP channel dormant refresh

> Owned by `vvip-channel-enablement`. Re-engage a dormant VVIP relationship — usually because the gatekeeper transitioned out of the role or the household / office hasn't been touched in 12+ months.

## When to run it

- Gatekeeper has been silent for 6+ months despite reasonable cadence touches
- Gatekeeper is known to have moved roles (transitioned, retired, reassigned)
- A new launch creates a natural reason to refresh the relationship
- Annual review surfaces relationships at risk of slipping from `dormant` to no-longer-active

## Diagnose first

VVIP dormancy almost always has a structural cause. Identify it before acting:

1. **Has the gatekeeper transitioned?** Most common cause. The relationship needs reintroduction through the new gatekeeper, not silent continuation.
2. **Has the principal transitioned?** If the principal retired, reassigned, or — in the case of households — the relationship was specifically with one member who is no longer in that role, the relationship may need to be re-evaluated entirely.
3. **Is the relationship paused for a known reason?** Some VVIP relationships go quiet around political transitions, household events, mourning periods, regional conflicts. These pauses are not dormancy; they're respect.
4. **Was there a protocol error?** A small protocol error can cause a quiet withdrawal that looks like dormancy. Honest reckoning required.
5. **Was reciprocity neglected?** Did we miss an event we should have attended, fail to congratulate on a national / household occasion, or fail to reciprocate an introduction? Silent debt accumulates.
6. **Has the household / office's interest changed?** A ruling family member who was active in real-estate patronage may have shifted to other priorities.

## Target outcome

Within six months:
- Current gatekeeper identified and communicating
- One protocol-correct re-engagement touch (often a national-day acknowledgment or a project-milestone reveal)
- Status updated to `active` (re-engaging) OR confirmed paused for a known reason OR confirmed off-boarded with respectful documentation
- Reciprocity ledger refreshed

## Success criteria

- Current gatekeeper identified and reachable
- Acknowledgment of refresh touch through gatekeeper (even a brief acknowledgment is success)
- Registry status updated (no orphan entries)
- Reciprocity ledger reflects current effort

## Anti-patterns

- **Pretending the gatekeeper hasn't changed.** Continuing to send to the old gatekeeper after they've transitioned creates two problems: the old gatekeeper sees inappropriate continued contact, and the new gatekeeper has no relationship.
- **Rushing the refresh.** VVIP refreshes that take 2–4 weeks read as transactional. Right cadence is months, with patience.
- **Defensive acknowledgment of past errors.** If a protocol error is the cause, the acknowledgment is short, clean, principal-signed, no over-explanation. "We acknowledge our oversight regarding [specific event] and remain at Your Highness's / Your Excellency's service" — not paragraphs of self-justification.
- **Generic refresh pack.** The wealth-channel anti-pattern applies more strongly here. The pack is bespoke or doesn't exist.
- **Public mention of the refresh.** Same discretion stance. No press, no social, no marketing reference.
- **Confusing dormancy with off-boarding.** Some relationships should be retired with respect — better to confirm off-boarded than to keep refreshing what isn't there.

## Variants

- **Gatekeeper transitioned, principal stable:** treat as re-introduction. New gatekeeper, fresh letter, principal-led, as if first contact.
- **Principal transitioned, household stable:** evaluate whether the household-level relationship transfers. Sometimes yes, often no — a new principal may want to choose their own counterparties.
- **Long pause with known reason (household event, political transition, mourning):** refresh respectfully, on the *household's* clock, not ours. Often the right action is to wait, send appropriate-occasion correspondence, and let them re-initiate.
- **Confirmed off-boarding:** when the relationship has clearly ended, document respectfully in registry, archive screening file, do not pretend otherwise. Honesty preserves the option to re-engage later if circumstances change.
