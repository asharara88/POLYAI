# Play — VVIP channel cultivation

> Owned by `vvip-channel-enablement`. Initiate a new VVIP relationship — ruling-family principal, minister, ambassador, sovereign-institution principal — through senior introduction, gatekeeper-mediated, with absolute discretion.

## When to run it

- Channel-development KPI calls for new VVIP cultivation in this period
- A specific VVIP relationship would create strategic value for the developer (brand prestige, government goodwill, ribbon-cutting, future-opportunity access)
- A senior-introduction path has become available (e.g. mutual relationship offers to introduce)

This is a long-cycle play. "Within one quarter" is often not the right time horizon. Annual is more realistic.

## Diagnose first

Do not skip these:

1. **Is the relationship strategic?** What does it accomplish for the developer beyond a possible (rare) transaction? Brand prestige with the household? Patronage of a project? Government relations? Future opportunities? If you can't articulate the strategic value clearly, stop — VVIP cultivation is too expensive in time and discretion to do speculatively.
2. **Is the introduction path real?** A "we can introduce" signal from a mutual relationship needs verification. Soft introductions die without follow-through.
3. **PEP / sanctions / FCPA screening — clean?** This must clear before any commercial discussion. Run on entry, before the first meeting.
4. **Is our principal ready?** VVIP cultivation is principal-to-principal. The developer's principal commits time. Confirm willingness and availability before activating the introduction.
5. **Has the gatekeeper been identified?** Every VVIP has a gatekeeper (protocol office, principal secretary, head of office, designated liaison). Cultivation runs through them. Identify before approaching.
6. **Reciprocity capacity?** What can the developer offer over years? Patronage of a cause, attendance at their events, alignment on shared priorities. Without reciprocity capacity the cultivation hollows out.

## Target outcome

Within twelve months:
- Confirmed gatekeeper relationship (named, current, communicating)
- One in-person principal-to-principal interaction (often at our sales gallery off-hours, their office, or a curated event)
- Counterparty registered as `cultivating` with full protocol notes
- PEP / sanctions / FCPA screening current
- Reciprocity ledger initiated
- No protocol errors recorded

Transactional outcome (a purchase) is a possible bonus, not the goal.

## Success criteria

- Letter received, acknowledged through gatekeeper
- Meeting held within reasonable window (don't measure in weeks; their calendar is sovereign)
- Status registered as `cultivating`, with named gatekeeper, language, and protocol notes
- Zero protocol errors in any interaction
- Reciprocity ledger has at least one entry within 12 months

## Anti-patterns

- **Cold approach.** Approaching a VVIP without a senior introduction is not just ineffective — it can be insulting to the principal and the household.
- **Bypassing the gatekeeper.** The gatekeeper relationship *is* the operable relationship. Bypassing them ends the cultivation immediately.
- **Commercial on the first touch.** Bringing a unit, a price, an allocation request, or any deal language to a cultivation meeting is a serious error.
- **Public reference.** Mentioning the relationship to anyone outside the developer's most senior team is a discretion failure. Absolute no-mention default.
- **Pressure.** Following up aggressively reads as need. VVIP cultivation runs on the counterparty's clock.
- **Skipping PEP / sanctions screening.** The risk is asymmetric: discovering an issue after-the-fact is much worse than discovering it before-the-fact.
- **Junior representation.** Sending anyone other than the developer's principal to a first meeting tells the VVIP they're not important.
- **Title errors.** "His Highness" vs. "His Royal Highness" matters. So does "His Excellency" vs. "The Right Honourable." Verify, never guess.

## Variants

- **Foreign dignitary visiting UAE:** narrower window — they're available for a defined period. Coordinate via their embassy's protocol officer.
- **Sovereign institution principal:** the relationship is institutional — building trust with the principal's office matters as much as the principal personally. Multi-touch.
- **Relationship-via-philanthropy:** sometimes the right cultivation path is shared patronage of a cause the VVIP cares about — joint contribution to a foundation, attendance at their causes' events. Slow, but durable.
- **Cultivation-without-meeting:** for some counterparties (heads of state, certain royals) a personal meeting may not happen for years. The relationship is sustained through correspondence on national / personal occasions and by reputation.
