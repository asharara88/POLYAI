# Play — Wealth channel reactivation

> Owned by `wealth-channel-enablement`. Re-engage a private banker, family-office principal, or independent introducer who has gone silent for 6+ months.

## When to run it

- Wealth-channel relationship has not engaged on any introduction or invitation in 6+ months
- A new launch is approaching where this relationship was previously productive
- Quarterly channel-development KPI calls for reactivation

## Diagnose first

Wealth-channel dormancy almost always has a specific structural cause — diagnose, don't guess:

1. **Has the relationship-owner moved roles?** Private bankers transition; family-office principals retire or hand off. The relationship may need to migrate to a new individual.
2. **Did the macro thesis shift?** If they were buying for currency-hedge reasons during a specific window and the window closed, dormancy is rational. A new thesis re-opens the relationship.
3. **Was the prior introduction a bad outcome?** A buyer they introduced may have had a bad experience (handover delay, unit issue, payment-plan friction). They won't introduce again until they're confident in resolution.
4. **Is our offering still in their portfolio thesis?** If they've shifted from real-estate to private credit, no amount of reactivation works on the same offering.
5. **Did we under-deliver on reciprocity?** Did they introduce, did we acknowledge, did we reciprocate appropriately when invited to their events? Silent debt accumulates.

## Target outcome

Within one quarter:
- Confirmed status of the relationship-owner (still in role, transitioned, or replaced)
- One scheduled meeting with current relationship-owner (in person preferred)
- Refreshed pack received and acknowledged
- ≥1 introduction in the next 6 months OR clean confirmation that the relationship is paused (not dormant) for a stated reason

## Sequence

| Step | Owner | Action | Approval |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | wealth-channel-enablement | Pull last 3 engagements; identify cause hypothesis | — |
| 2 | wealth-channel-enablement | Verify current relationship-owner via mutual relationship / public LinkedIn / firm directory | — |
| 3 | wealth-channel-enablement | If the role transitioned: re-introduction play, not reactivation play (different sequence) | — |
| 4 | wealth-channel-enablement | Refresh the principal pack — current launch context, current thesis, named partners, IRR + sensitivity | brand-design + compliance |

| 9 | wealth-channel-enablement | At 90 days: assess — introductions made, positioned for future, or confirmed paused | — |

## Success criteria

- Meeting held in person within 30 days of outreach
- Relationship status updated in registry (active / paused / off-boarded with reason)
- If active: ≥1 introduction within 6 months
- Reciprocity ledger updated

## Anti-patterns

- **Bulk reactivation email.** Lethal in this channel. One impersonal touch can end the relationship.
- **Skipping the diagnosis.** Reactivating without understanding why they went silent often produces a polite "still thinking about it" loop forever.
- **Pressure tactics.** "Last chance to introduce before allocation closes" reads as desperate and ends trust.
- **Using the same pack as before.** They've seen it. The refresh has to be *different* — current thesis, new partner reveal, new construction milestone with verified evidence.
- **Bypassing the principal.** Sending a junior salesperson to a wealth-channel reactivation meeting tells them they're not important.

## Variants

- **Private bank — relationship-owner transitioned:** treat as new-relationship signing. Re-introduction through the bank's relationship-management head, not the prior banker.
- **Family office — principal retired:** the next-generation principal may have completely different thesis. Don't assume the prior relationship transfers.
- **Independent introducer who closed:** sometimes the introducer firm closed; the principals may have moved to a different firm. Track them, not the firm.
- **Reactivation in a new launch:** if dormancy was launch-fit-driven, a new launch with different fit is a clean re-introduction angle.
